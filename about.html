About our Organisation

If a country’s rural education sector is working well, it reflects on the entire economic state of a country. Here is how we aim to upgrade the rural education scenario in India, especially for women, who have loads of expertise in making artisan products, but have poor knowledge on the means of selling the same. Through this platform, we aim to impart them with the technical knowledge of the means of using internet to their advantage and along with that, we help them in reaching out to potential customers avoiding any language barriers.
